How to Play

At the beginning you have to place all ships. You can rotate
the ships by clicking on the box that has the end of the ship
in it. Make sure that all ships are at least one space apart
(edge ​​excluded). When you have placed all the ships you can
press the commit button to submit the positions

If everything is correct there will be a loading screen. Then
wait until the other player has also given up his ships

then you play rock paper scissors to choose who goes first
regulate:
Scissors => paper
Paper => stone
Rock => scissors

Above you can see how many times a shot has already been
taken, if the background of this field is red it is the
opponent's turn, if it is green it is your turn

If you have shot, this field turns red, if this field is
transparent red then you didn't hit, but you can clearly see
that you hit

You have to shoot into the right field, in the left you can
see your ships and where your opponent has already shot
